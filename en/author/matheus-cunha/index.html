Heelloo!

I’m Matheus Cunha (or just macunha). 👋

I love open-source software and the distribution of knowledge (and power) that happened as a consequence of the Free Software Foundation and Linux.

As a consequence of that I happened to be involved into DevOps projects using Linux and open-source tools, Agile software development, Cloud Computing, and Data Engineering of cloud-first Data Lakes.

I’ve worked for various businesses, architecting and implementing highly available and resilient Cloud systems, helping them to be more future-proof and competitive in a highly changing market. Not just improving their technical stack but also the developer experience and productivity.

During these projects, I had the chance to launch software in all major (public) cloud providers, including sometimes multi-cloud or hybrid (on-premises private+public) approaches.

I’m always looking for ways to improve (Kaizen 🙂), learn more, and move further. Aiming to achieve a higher potential for the businesses and projects I got involved in.
